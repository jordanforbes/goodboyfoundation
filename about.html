The Good Boy Foundation

In a world of dogs without enough treats, there needs to be a saviour. All good boys deserve more treats! That is our mission at 'The Good Boy Foundation.'

Donate

"The Good Boy Foundation" takes in many special needs animals.
We take care of any snacks and treats of any animal we rescue including,
but not limited to: bacon bits, diced cat organs, scooby snacks, your dogs personal vomit, another dogs vomit, ham, fried tuna, t-bone steak, a bone etc. On average, GBF takes in at least five animals per week that likes some of the snacks listed above, or have not been given enough treats to ensure happiness.

We do not believe that any animal should be left to starve from lack of treats because their owner prefers dog food over tasty snacks. Society has already failed them at one point in time and someone needs to speak up and rescue those that are defenseless and without a voice. That is who we are are and that is what The Good Boy Foundation has always been about. These treats and snacks are extremely expensive and we need the communities help to not leave these poor souls to be discarded with no one to care for them
